Strategic Product Placement Analysis

Analyzing how brand placement in movies and digital media influences consumer behavior and product sales

Project Story

Product placement has become one of the most powerful marketing strategies in modern media. Brands collaborate with movies, television shows and digital platforms to integrate their products naturally into entertainment content.

This project focuses on analyzing how product placement affects customer perception, brand awareness and purchasing behavior by studying marketing data and media exposure.

Using Tableau data visualization dashboards, we explore trends in brand appearances, campaign effectiveness and audience engagement to help businesses design better product placement strategies.
