About the Project

Strategic Product Placement Analysis

Strategic product placement has become a key marketing technique used by brands to increase visibility and influence consumer purchasing decisions. Companies collaborate with movies, television shows, streaming platforms, and digital creators to integrate their products naturally within entertainment content.

This project analyzes the impact of product placements on brand awareness, consumer perception, and product sales. By using data analytics and visualization techniques, the study helps identify patterns in media exposure and marketing performance.

Using interactive dashboards created in Tableau, this project visualizes important metrics such as campaign effectiveness, brand appearances, and sales growth trends. These insights help marketers develop more effective product placement strategies for future campaigns.

Data Visualization

Interactive dashboards help explore marketing trends and campaign performance.

Marketing Insights

Provides insights into how product placements influence brand awareness.

Sales Analysis

Evaluates the relationship between media exposure and product sales growth.

Tools & Technologies Used

  • Tableau – Data Visualization and Dashboard Creation
  • HTML, CSS, Bootstrap – Web Interface Design
  • JavaScript – Interactive Features and Animations
  • Data Analytics – Marketing and Consumer Behavior Analysis
